Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our balloon artistry and stage setup services

What types of events do you provide balloon artistry & stage setup for?

We provide for any celebratory event like birthdays, baby showers, proposals, brand campaigns etc.

How far in advance should I book your services for my event?

Book 2 weeks before your event to make payment a week or earlier before your event.

Can you assist with event planning and design?

UNFORTUNATELY, we do not plan and design your event.

Do you handle the setup and takedown of the balloons and stage setup?

Yes, we do. After the event, the balloons are yours to keep.

Do you provide delivery services for the balloons and stage setup?

Yes, we do. All deliveries in a 15 km radius cost R350.00 and outside the radius at a cost of R550.00.

What payment option do you prefer, and do you require a deposit?

We prefer bank transfer. A 75% deposit of the total amount is required a week before the event and the remaining 25% two days before the event.

How do I go about requesting a quote?

Once we have consulted on how you want the stage setup to look like.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 2 days before the event will result in the forfeiture of the 75% deposit. Cancellations made before 7 days after payment will result in a refund of 50% of your deposit.

Do you offer additional services such as lighting, floral arrangement etc?

Yes, at an additional cost, because we'd use other service providers.
